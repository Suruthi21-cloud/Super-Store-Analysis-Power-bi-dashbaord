# Super-Store-Analysis-Power-bi-dashbaord

📊 Super Store Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

**Project Overview**
This Power BI project analyzes Super Store sales data to understand business performance, identify sales trends, evaluate regional and category performance, and generate actionable business insights. The project includes an interactive dashboard, an executive summary, and a business storyboard to support data-driven decision-making.

**Project Components**
📊 Interactive Dashboard
Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to monitor key business metrics.
Created KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, and Total Customers.
Analyzed monthly sales trends, regional sales performance, category-wise sales, and customer segment profitability.
Added slicers for dynamic filtering and better user interaction.

📝 Executive Summary
Summarized overall business performance using key KPIs.
Highlighted major business insights from the analysis.
Provided actionable business recommendations to improve sales and profitability.

📖 Business Storyboard
Presented the analysis as a business story, starting from the business problem through data analysis to final recommendations.
Explained key findings using dashboard visuals and business insights.
Demonstrated how data can support strategic business decisions.

**Key Insights**
Technology category generated the highest sales.
West region recorded the strongest sales performance.
Consumer segment contributed the highest profit.
Sales peaked during November, indicating seasonal demand.

**Business Recommendations**
Increase inventory for high-demand Technology products.
Focus marketing efforts on lower-performing regions.
Improve engagement with lower-profit customer segments.
Continue promotional campaigns during peak sales periods.
